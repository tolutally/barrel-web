# Image assets

The "Who it's for" cards on `index.html` expect two photos in this folder.
Until they exist, the cards show a neutral hatched slot instead of a broken image.

| File | Card | What to pick |
|---|---|---|
| `import-trade.jpg` | Importing & paying out (ink card) | A real photo with a person: warehouse receiving, checking inbound boxes/pallets, inspecting a shipment with a laptop or clipboard. |
| `export-trade.jpg` | Exporting & getting paid (lime card) | A real photo with a person: packing/labeling goods for shipment, forklift or dock worker at a container yard or port. |

Guidelines:
- Real photography only (e.g. Unsplash or Pexels free licenses) — no AI-generated imagery.
- Must include a human element (a person working, hands packing, etc.).
- Landscape orientation, at least 1200px wide; the slot crops to roughly 2:1 with `object-fit: cover`.
- Warm, natural tones sit best against the paper/ink/lime palette.

Suggested searches:
- https://unsplash.com/s/photos/warehouse-worker
- https://unsplash.com/s/photos/shipping-containers-worker
- https://www.pexels.com/search/warehouse%20worker/
- https://www.pexels.com/search/cargo%20port%20worker/
